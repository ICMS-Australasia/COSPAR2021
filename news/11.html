Meet the Women of COSPAR 2020

COSPAR has a major focus on equity and diversity. Accordingly, this is the first COSPAR Assembly with limits on the number of abstracts that a person can submit or present at the Assembly, as well as the number of solicited presentations a person can make. We are specifically trying to increase the number and profile of presentations by younger and more diverse people, so as to give people more opportunities and grow the global space research community.
