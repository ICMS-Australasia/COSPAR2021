COSPAR-K is an interactive event that will be held in conjunction with COSPAR 2020.

The event deck at the COSPAR 2020 venue in Sydney will hold a series of interactive experiences and activities structured into three-hour blocks for school groups to engage with. The STEM ED Committee is collaborating with pre-eminent museums and education facilities, as well as space industry organisations which facilitate STEM education.

We had some very special guests on our stand at the recent 8th Space Forum held in Adelaide, South Australia. Rod Drury (pictured) from Lockheed Martin, joined Head of the Australian Space Agency, Dr Megan Clark AC and Keynote Speaker Luca Del Monte, Head of Industrial Policy and SME Division from the European Space Agency.

Earlier in the day, all had participated in the scientific program but their afternoon was spent in traditional Australian style, networking with the locals!

This week Team Australia will be at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, exhibiting at the International Astronautical Congress - IAC 2019 Congress. Come visit the COSPAR 2020 stand (#575) – you never know who you might meet!

Meet our Interdisciplinary Speakers

Over the coming months each COSPAR 2020 e-newsletter will feature our Keynote Speakers. In this issue we are proud to introduce Hitoshi Kuninaka and Michele Dougherty who head-line the COSPAR 2020 stellar line-up.

Hitoshi Kuninaka, Director General, Institute of Space and Astronautical Science, Vice President, Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency. His abstract The Status of Hayabusa 2 Asteroid Explorer and Its Results on the COSPAR 2020 website.

Michele Dougherty is Head of the Physics Department at Imperial College London and a Fellow of the Royal Society. Her abstract on New aspects of the magnetospheres of Jupiter and Saturn can be viewed online.
