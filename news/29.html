COSPAR-2021-Hybrid Presenter Information

Congratulations, you will have the honour of being the first virtual presenters of a COSPAR-Hybrid Assembly.

You are pioneering the path for future Assemblies and contributing to space research advancement, truly connecting space for global impact.

We have also created an instructional video that will assist you in preparing your submission.

Presenters are required to upload their three minute video, at a minimum, by Monday 30 November 2020 to secure the Presenter Early Bird Rate. This video is required and will be streamed as your presentation within your Session.

Your full presentation will be due by 30 December 2020.

Instructions for accessing the COSPAR-2021-Hybrid Speaker Platform, joining your session, participating in live Q&A and more will be available in due course.

Register Now

The entire program for COSPAR-2021-Hybrid, will be pre-recorded and the whole program assembled before 28 January will be available to be viewed at each participant’s convenience, through to the end of 2021.

A presenter-only fee of €440 represents a 45% reduction with respect to the full registration fee! This fee is only available until 30 November 2020 with the upload of your presentation.

The Hybrid early bird rate has been reduced to €540 (from €680 initially) with an extended deadline until 31 December 2020, thus offering over 30% in savings to the full fee.

The new format will offer a dynamic and immersive experience for all delegates.
