We in the Southern Hemisphere wanted to take some time just to say Thank You and acknowledge that the Thanks Giving period holds a significant place on the North American calendar.

With this in mind, we are giving our COSPAR-Hybrid-2021 presenters an extension to 7 December 2020 to submit your short summary video.

As another treat we present an extended peek of Michele Dougherty, one of the COSPAR-2021-Hybrid Interdisciplinary Speakers, who will be speaking on the new aspects of the magnetospheres of Jupiter and Saturn.

Happy Thanksgiving! We hope that the holiday season brings joy, warmth and laughter to you and your loved ones.
