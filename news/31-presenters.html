As we hurtle towards the Early Bird Registration deadline of 30 November 2020, we are excited to announce a special bonus event that will be available to all early bird presenter registrants to COSPAR-2021-Hybrid.

Register and submit your video by 30 November and you will receive admission to a one-off special Assembly pre-event featuring three of our Interdisciplinary Speakers.

Marica Branchesi

Gran Sasso Science Institute and INFN-LNGS, L'Aquila, Italy
New High Energy Astrophysics Involving Gravitational Waves and Satellite Observations

Michele Dougherty

Professor of Space Physics at Imperial College London (UK)
New Aspects of the Magnetospheres of Jupiter and Saturn

John Church

University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia
Climate Change, the Ocean and Cryosphere

Each of the Interdisciplinary Speakers will provide a presentation, plus some additional insights that will only be available at this pre-event.

Your access key will be provided when you complete the registration process. This offer is only available until 30 November 2020.

If you are already one of the 400 registered delegates, you will be sent your access key prior to the event launch date.
