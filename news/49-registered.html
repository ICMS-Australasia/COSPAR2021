Full Throttle Forward

In preparation for the opening day of COSPAR 2021 on Friday 29 January 2021. We want to share with you the first glimpse of the Virtual Platform and share with you some of the special features that will highlight the scientific program, segmented into specific Commission streams, daily IDL sessions and the Poster Gallery. There are live, simu-live and on-demand webcasts with all sessions providing live Q&A, either through talking heads or chat box functionality. In addition, value added events such as the Space Agency Leaders RoundTable and Lockheed Martin’s cross-disciplinary workshops are available.

Features of the COSPAR Platform

All 600 sessions will be streamed on the Platform and will then be available on-demand to registered delegates. That’s 2,070 videos of scientific content! Mark your diaries as the official launch to access the Platform is Thursday 27 January 2021, to give you time to set-up your profile and to familiarise yourself with the Platform, ensuring optimal interaction.

For 80% of participants who have shared their details with us, we have preloaded the information you have provided so you will only need to create your profile picture or business avatar! You will also be able to set your profile your local time zone, remember the Program is shown in AEDT – Australian Eastern Daylight Saving Time.

At this time, the COSPAR 2021 App will be ready to download from the App store. The App will be a very useful tool for our participant’s on the go.

Following are only a few of the features available; you will discover many more and we look forward to seeing in the COSPAR 2021 corridors to facilitate networking.

Help and Support 24/7

During the Assembly the Help Desk will be staffed 24/7 to assist with live participant technical support and FAQs. There is also a fully staffed Resource Centre who will support your needs whilst at the Assembly. The Resource Centre will be the content repository for each attendee to utilise until the end of 2021.

Networking and Connecting

We know networking and connecting with colleagues is high on your Agenda and virtual experience for COSPAR 2021. To facilitate your important conversations we have created a dedicated Networking Lounge.

Private chat rooms for one on one discussions are also possible. Grab a virtual coffee (or beer) and enjoy the company of an old acquaintance or new friend.

Time between sessions

Allow time in between sessions to walk the halls of the exhibition. Here you will be able to meet with key industry organisations, such as the Anchor Sponsor, Lockheed Martin, but also others who are keen to meet with you and discuss their organisational inputs into the space sector.

COSPAR-K is for the whole family!

COSPAR-K is the event the whole family can enjoy! Running alongside the grown-ups event is COSPAR-K, aimed at students of Science Technology Engineering and Maths, there are seven virtual excursions for schoolchildren to enjoy. Also for your family and friends to discover, there is a Public Lecture on Tuesday 2 February, with Dr Adrian Brown.

We look forward to sharing the platform and access instructions next week, until then we trust that you have booked your time in your daily calendar and have set yourself up in a comfortable area, with a big screen and lots of drinks and snacks!

There is a world of space research to enjoy, coming soon.
