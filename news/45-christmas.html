Christmas in Australia

With the holiday season fast approaching we take this opportunity to wish you all a safe and enjoyable festive season. December in the Southern Hemisphere is a little different than for our friends closer to the North Pole. We realise the impact COVID-19 has had on us all and that for most Australians (even with border and travel restictions!), we will celebrate this holiday together. For many the year means you’ve been kept out of your lab, you’ve worked from home and you have adapted to the times we currently face! We thank you for your support of COSPAR-2021-Hybrid.

For our friends outside of Australia, Santa sometimes gives the reindeers a rest and might use kangaroos, or boomers as they are colloquially called in Australia, to deliver his gifts and cheer! He also changes his clothes for the weather to allow for the warmer temperatures. And instead of a hot beverage during his stop-off, a nice cold beer is left for his refreshment!

Here are five things we in Australia typically enjoy during our summer season.

Spending time at the beach. Sydney is well known for it’s beaches and the iconic Bondi Beach especially.
Christmas Lunch features fresh seafood, cold meats and salads.
Boxing Day games of backyard cricket or a Test Match that is traditional in Australia
New Year’s Eve fireworks spectacular on Sydney Harbour can now be viewed by over one billion people worldwide
Enjoying a New Year’s Day BBQ in the park

Whatever you and your loved ones are doing throughout the festive period we would like to wish you a happy end to 2020 and a more successful New Year. We look forward to seeing most of you, virtually, in 2021.

Thanks For Registering and being a part of COSPAR-2021-Hybrid

We have almost 2,100 videos submitted, which will form part of the sessions you will participate in. Here is a list of your benefits so far, for registering!

  • Live Q&A is included on the IDL's and additional webcasts for the selected presenters to engage in a discussion.
  • For you, the registered delegate, you can acess you the preliminary bonus recordings of the IDL speakers’. The password has been sent to you already, contact us if you can’t find it.
  • Each of the sessions will be created to run highly engaging audio and video webcasting presentations in either a Live, Simulated-Live, or On-Demand webcast, with phone audio or webcam presenters in a single source display, for up to 90 mins in length, with unlimited on-demand viewing for 12 months.
  • Live Q&A sessions will be moderated by the Session Chairs who will in turn direct each question to the speakers. This is via text chat directly to the Moderator.
  • The platform will facilitate alternate Live video Q&A with On-Demand and scheduled Breakout rooms to complement the virtual experience.
  • We have confirmed Live sessions with Q&A are recorded and available as part of the On-Demand sessions.
  • Each session of 90 minutes will alternate between live video Q&A and live (text-based) Q&A for optimum engagement and knowledge sharing between delegates and time zones.
  • Simultaneously, the platform will deliver corridor conversations via Live 1:1 video chat; available 24/7 for up to 30mins to encourage collaboration.
  • In addition to the above sessions, participants can attend the sponsor halls and poster gallery
