COSPAR-2021-Hybrid; where resources and space converge


Participate via submitting an abstract by 5 October 2020

Space-based remote sensing systems coupled with advanced data analytics are already playing an enormous role in turning space-derived data into solutions on Earth, for example for the resources sector and this will only increase. Remote robotic capabilities derived from the resources sectorâ€™s remote asset management capabilities, will play critical roles as we utilise space more and more and as we explore and extend our reach deep into the solar system.

COSPAR 2021 will provide the ideal forum for conversation and new co-operations, in this overlap region with space research and innovation.

Space is not a discipline, but rather, a complex and challenging domain in which we deploy technologies and activities to deepen our understanding of the planet we live on, to help us meet the significant needs and challenges faced by the international community each day and to create opportunities that pull us forwards.
