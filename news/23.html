The COSPAR-2021-Hybrid Promise

With the dynamic, energetic and re-imagined COSPAR-2021-Hybrid we know the Assembly will be able to deliver on all the COSPAR Assembly goals, and more!

A Hybrid Assembly lowers cost and time savings with no travel or accommodation expenses. The new format is especially designed for COSPAR attendees, who are in the business of exploration. It’s a significant reason alone to register for COSPAR-2021-Hybrid!

So, why choose COSPAR-2021-Hybrid over a galaxy of conferencing choices? CONTENT. No other conference will provide:

  • Access to over 1,000+ hours of space research on-demand and live. ALL content as you require, downloadable until the end of 2021. Stored and viewable on your device for as long as you need. This allows ample time to view every session presented at COSPAR-2021-Hybrid and re-watch any sessions.
  • Stream live or watch content on-demand when it fits into your schedule and learn at your own pace, in your own style.
  • Access to 100% of the scientific program. In comparison to in-person only, the average amount of sessions an in-person delegate would normally be able to attend is approximately 3 - 5 sessions per hour. We’ve estimated this is only 8% of the scientific program. A 92% gain!

The COSPAR-2021-Hybrid Assembly also offers new opportunities to network and engage.

  • Attendees can chat with internal and external colleagues continually during live stream presentations to share insights and observations relevant to your research.
  • On-line engagement opportunities with global space industry personnel, government agencies and commercial representatives from sponsoring and exhibiting organisations from all over the world.
  • COSPAR-2021-Hybrid is more accessible to delegates from around the world with travel/visa or physical limitation issues who would not usually be able to attend a COSPAR Assembly.
  • Continue your lifelong learning journey. Pique your interest with other areas of research that you would not otherwise have the time to attend
