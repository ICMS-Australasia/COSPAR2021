Australia’s Parkes radio telescope – affectionately known to us Aussie’s as “The Dish” famously supported receiving the television signals on that momentous day.

And we haven’t stopped communicating with the world ever since. It is in this spirit that COSPAR 2020’s mission is to share with the world the knowledge, technology and ideology grown through our COSPAR community.

COSPAR - What a Week

To best communicate and share this knowledge the COSPAR team are putting together a week-long program of cutting edge content in the core scientific program, ambitious outreach to the public and STEM students to participate through COSPAR-X and collaborative engagement with our key stakeholders and supports such as our Anchor Sponsor Lockheed Martin.

The whole of the science community is behind us with National Science Week taking place the week of the Assembly providing further outreach and connectivity with the future of our industry. You can review what we are planning here.

Key dates

So mark your diaries for next month 15 August when registration opens (12 months before COSPAR). You can also submit your abstract from 19 August 2019 and be a part of the scientific program.

Until then, you can review additional information about the scientific program.

Koala Competition – Next Stop Singapore

Another way for you to attend is to win a free registration! In the lead, up to COSPAR 2020, the Organising Committee and its Ambassadors will be distributing koalas worldwide. You will need get your hands on one of these furry friends, as they are the key to a possible free registration.

Later this week we will be in Singapore at the Asia Oceania Geosciences Society (AOGS) Conference 28 July to 2 August. Make sure you check-in at Booth A12 - the COSPAR 2020 stand in the exhibition hall. We have daily competitions, giveaways and the all-important Clip-On Koala Giveaway.

Koalas will also be available at the International Astronautical Congress 2019 in Washington, DC in October and in Herzliya, Israel at the 4th COSPAR Symposium in November 2019.

For details on how to enter our competition, see our competition here.
