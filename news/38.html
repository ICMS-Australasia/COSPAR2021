A Message from
Michele Dougherty

COSPAR-2021-Hybrid
Interdisciplinary Speaker

Michele Dougherty, who will be speaking on the new aspects of the magnetospheres of Jupiter and Saturn, will deliver a special presentation that will only be available to all Early Bird registrants to COSPAR-2021-Hybrid. Registering by 30 November 2020 will provide you with this access.

This special session will also include presentations by:

Marica Branchesi

Gran Sasso Science Institute and INFN-LNGS, L'Aquila, Italy
New High Energy Astrophysics Involving Gravitational Waves and Satellite Observations

John Church

University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia
Climate Change, the Ocean and Cryosphere

Each of the Interdisciplinary Speakers will provide a presentation overview, plus some additional insights that will only be available at this pre-event. Your access key will be provided when you complete the registration process. This offer is only available until 30 November 2020.
