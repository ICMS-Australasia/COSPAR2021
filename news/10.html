MECO “Main Engine Cut-Off” 14/02/2020

The countdown is on! There is only two weeks to go to submit your abstract for the largest space research Assembly on the planet. Submit your abstract to secure your place at COSPAR 2020.

Submit your Abstract

The COSPAR 2020 Abstract submission deadline is closing at midnight Friday 14 February 2020 (AEST).

All presenters must register in order to attend the Scientific Assembly.

The abstract submission fee does not register you for the Scientific Assembly for which separate registration fees must be paid.

Koala Competition Winner Announcement

We are pleased to announce that Siti Harwani Md Yusoff is the winner of the COSPAR 2020 Koala Competition. The koalas for the winning picture were sent to a 20 km height using a High Altitude Balloon during an experiment for a near-space system. Vision is of Kampar, Perak, Malaysia.

Siti’s prize includes a complimentary full delegate registration to COSPAR 2020 and five night’s accommodation at at the Hotel Park Royal, Darling Harbour.

The top 12 submissions shortlisted can be viewed in the Koala Competition Gallery.
