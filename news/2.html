The COSPAR 2018 gathering in Pasadena was a huge success and the Sydney Team congratulate the Committee and Organisers for their work in celebrating and bringing to life the 60th anniversary celebrations. Pasadena, USA certainly held true to those traditions.

The COSPAR 2020 Team had a terrific time in Pasadena and met over a third of the attendees in person. Thank to all who visited our stand. For those who missed seeing us there are plenty of opportunities on the horizon: future stops include Bremen, Germany at the IAC in October 2018 and Herzliya, Israel at the 4th COSPAR Symposium in November 2019.

Sydney, Australia, is one of the world’s most picturesque cities. Surrounded by a majestic harbour and scattered with beaches, national parks and green areas, this is a city that entices you to explore.

Please bookmark the dates of 15–23 August, 2020 in your calendar now. We encourage you to experience our unique Australian hospitality. Your journey to Australia will be more than worthwhile professionally and personally.

At Mission Control, COSPAR 2020 is led by our Assembly Chair, Professor Russell Boyce, UNSW Canberra. He is supported by a dedicated group of volunteer professionals who are determined to ensure that your experience in Sydney will be out of this world. On-board we have Iver Cairns, University of Sydney, COSPAR 2020 Scientific Program Committee Chair, Fred Menk, Chair, National Committee for Space and Radio Science and Imogen Ray, immediate past President of the Australian Youth Aerospace Association.

Supporting our endeavours are the COSPAR team including President Len Fisk, Executive Director Jean-Louis Fellous, Associate Director Aaron Janofsky, Administrative Assistant Leigh Fergus, and the Program Committee comprised of the chairs of COSPAR Scientific Commissions and Panels.

The theme of the 2020 Assembly is Connecting Space Research for Global Impact and Australia stands to leverage hosting the Assembly to provide a report card on the momentum of the newly created Australian Space Agency.

In the lead up to COSPAR 2020 the Organising Committee and its Ambassadors will be distributing koalas worldwide.

Next month we are off to the International Astronautical Congress (IAC 2018) in Bremen, Germany. Make sure you dock at the Australian Space Agency Booth in the exhibition hall. We have daily competitions, give-aways and the all important Clip-On Koala Giveaway. Entries have already started jumping in, such as this entry from Rosaly Lopes titled Koala on Mars.

You have plenty of time to execute your mission!
