With the holiday season fast approaching we take this opportunity to wish you all a safe and enjoyable festive season. December in the Southern Hemisphere is a little different than for our friends closer to the North Pole. Santa sometimes gives the reindeers a rest and might use kangaroos, or boomers as they are colloquially called in Australia, to deliver his gifts and cheer! He also changes his clothes for the weather to allow for the warmer temperatures. And instead of a hot beverage during his stop-off, a nice cold beer is left for his refreshment!

Here are five things we in Australia typically enjoy during our summer season.

Spending time at the beach. Sydney is well known for it’s beaches and the iconic Bondi Beach especially.
Christmas Lunch features fresh seafood, cold meats and salads.
Boxing Day games of backyard cricket or a Test Match that is traditional in Australia
New Year’s Eve fireworks spectacular on Sydney Harbour which is now viewed by over one billion people worldwide
Enjoying a New Year’s Day BBQ in the park

Whatever you and your loved ones are doing throughout the festive period we would like to wish you a very happy end to 2019 and a successful New Year. We hope to see you in Sydney in August 2020!

Come down under and see the Southern Lights for yourself next year at COSPAR 2020!
